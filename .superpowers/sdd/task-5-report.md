- **Opening:** Reference specific regulation and deadline
- **Discovery goal:** Understand compliance requirement, deadline, current state, team capacity
- **5 discovery questions:**
  - Compliance requirement (which standard, what's driving it)
  - Timeline & current state (deadline, starting from scratch or partial)
  - Team & capability gap (hire, external help, upskill)
  - Decision-making & approval (who drives it, budget exists)
  - Success definition (pass audit, get cert, use as sales tool)
- **5-step call arc:** Research → Discovery → Probe Constraint → Suggest → Close

- **Success metrics** (compliance-focused outcomes)

#### Template 3: Breach Recovery Signal
- **Best for:** Post-incident hiring, rebuilding security function
- **Opening:** Sympathetic acknowledgment of incident, recognition of recovery effort (NOT opportunistic)
- **Discovery goal:** Understand incident scope, recovery stage, rebuild priorities, new leader's mandate, timeline
- **5 discovery questions:**
  - Incident scope & status (what happened, how long ago, still in crisis vs. rebuild)
  - Impact & regulatory fallout (customer impact, compliance obligations)
  - Leadership & mandate (new CISO, scope of rebuild, full function or focused area)
  - Rebuild priorities (prevent same thing, compliance posture, customer trust)
  - Timeline & pressure (board expectations, customer deadlines, regulatory timelines)
- **5-step call arc:** Acknowledge & Empathize → Discovery → Probe Rebuild Reality → Suggest Approach → Close
- **7 common objections** with responses (IR firm coverage, sensitivity about details, still in crisis, handle internally, sensitive internally, board validation, credibility question)
- **Customization tips** (reference specific incident, praise leadership decision, acknowledge recovery communication, reference regulatory implications, industry context)
- **Post-call workflow** (recovery-specific Airtable updates)
- **Success metrics** (recovery-focused outcomes)

#### Template 4: Generic/Unknown Signal
- **Best for:** Tier-1 role without clear signal identified (still valuable, more exploratory)
- **Opening:** Curiosity-based, acknowledge rarity of hire
- **Discovery goal:** Uncover the true WHY signal through discovery questions
- **5 discovery questions:**
  - Hiring driver (what triggered the decision now)
  - Current state & gap (building from scratch or scaling)
  - Pain point (what's keeping them up at night)
  - Decision-making (who's behind it, budget approved)
  - Success definition (first 90 days win conditions)
- **5-step call arc:** Acknowledge Rarity → Discovery → Probe Real Driver → Suggest Based on Need → Close
- **7 common objections** (not sure if need external help, figuring out internally, not on roadmap, already have partner, want to know about you, compare to competitors, need to loop in decision-maker)
- **Customization tips** (do homework before calling, company-specific context, lead with curiosity, industry knowledge bridge, reference hiring significance)
- **Post-call workflow** (update Why Signal based on discovery)
- **Success metrics** (generic/exploratory outcomes)
